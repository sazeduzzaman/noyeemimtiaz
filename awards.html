01 // ABOUT ME

Award-Winning Journey in
Directing & Storytelling

Noyeem Imtiaz Neamul is an acclaimed Bangladeshi filmmaker, director, and writer whose work has earned multiple prestigious awards, including the DCRU Award, the Cultural Journalists' Forum of Bangladesh (CJFB) Award 2010, and the Television Reports Unity of Bangladesh (TRUB) Award 2024 for Best Director.

Renowned for his emotional depth and creative storytelling, Noyeem’s projects span drama, film, and television. His award- winning drama “Sahara Desert” remains a milestone that highlights his commitment to impactful narratives and cinematic excellence.
