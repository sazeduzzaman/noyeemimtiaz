About Noyeem Imtiaz Neamul

Noyeem Imtiaz Neamul is a visionary filmmaker and performer from Bangladesh, known for his work as a Director, Writer, and Actor in films, dramas, and digital projects.

With a passion for storytelling, Noyeem blends creativity and emotion to craft experiences that resonate with audiences. His work spans television dramas, short films, and feature films, exploring social themes, human relationships, and compelling narratives. He has directed acclaimed projects like Ek Cup Cha, Chhere Jas Na, and Gangchil, and continues to innovate in both cinema and digital media.
