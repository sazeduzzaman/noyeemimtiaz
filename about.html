Years Of Experience in
Directing

Noyeem Imtiaz Neamul is a visionary filmmaker and performer from Bangladesh, known for his work as a Director, Writer, and Actor in films, dramas, and digital projects.

Noyeem Imtiaz Neamul is a Bangladeshi film director and screenwriter known for his contributions to contemporary Bangladeshi cinema. Based in Dhaka, he has directed several notable films that explore social themes and human relationships.

About Personal Life
Neyamul is married. His wife is an advocate. They have two sons and one daughter. He loves traveling and has visited many countries. In his leisure time, he enjoys reading books and watching movies.
Future Plans
Two short films are currently in production:
  • Under the Burqa
  • Identity Crisis
He also aspires to direct a full-length feature film in Hollywood in the future.
Filmmaking Style & Inspirations
Neyamul is primarily interested in off-track and thriller-based stories. As inspirations, he mentions:
  • Afzal Hossain (Bangladesh)
  • Rajkumar Hirani (India)
  • James Cameron (Hollywood)
  • Abbas Kiarostami (Iran)
Noyeem's Philosophy
We believe that great storytelling doesn’t just promote products—it builds brands and touches hearts. Our team of passionate creators, strategists, and media planners work relentlessly to bring aspirations to life through visual narratives.
Noyeem's Team
We are powered by a 15+ strong team, including: Brand Strategists, Media Planners, Creative Designers, Client Service Executives, Ground Campaign Experts
My Membership
Noyeem Imtiaz Neamul holds memberships in several prestigious organizations:
  • Director’s Guild (Membership No. 094)
  • Bangladesh Film Director Association
  • Bangladesh Film Producers Distributors Association
  • Television Programme Producers' Association of Bangladesh (TELEPAB)
  • Actors Equity Bangladesh
  • Member of Eastern India Motion Picture Association (EIMPA)
Additionally, he is the Secretary of Save the Rivers Bangladesh Society and an active member of cultural organizations such as Dhaka Theatre and Dhaka Sarakolpon.
